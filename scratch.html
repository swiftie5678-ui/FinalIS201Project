Consider the Lilies

Matthew 6:28-30

We are so grateful to know the symbolism behind the lilies of the field. Knowing that the Lord protects all of His creations helps us to know He protects us.

Through the Atonement of Jesus Christ our most important relationships can be forever. He is our personal and relational Redeemer.
